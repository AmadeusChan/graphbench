What is GraphBench?

Graph and irregular computation are of increasing importance to industry, the government, and the sciences. The core computational component of many “big data”, machine-learning, and security applications is an irregular computation typically done on a graph data-structure.

GraphBench's goal is be a curation of:

  • Graph analysis kernels
  • Reference sequential implementations
  • Graph data-sets and data-set generators
  • Specifications for standardized performance results

GraphBench is very much in progress and is meant to be a community effort. So please email us if you are willing to participate!
